Hello! I'm Lyon Huang, a product designer with a passion for building experiences that feel effortless. I thrive at the intersection of user empathy, systems thinking, and visual craft.

Most recently, I've been designing at Pluto TV, shaping UX for a streaming platform reaching millions of viewers. Before that, I spent years at Disney Parks designing mobile ordering experiences for Disneyland and Walt Disney World. I've also taken on freelance work in the health-tech space.

I'm currently based in Los Angeles and looking for my next opportunity to design products that genuinely matter.
